My Sports Interests

This is a page that describes my sports interests

I played Rugby from the age of 11 to the age of 16. I have always had an interest in it and always watch the Ireland matches.

I was on the bench for the winning Junior Cup Team in 2015 when I was 16. Following this, I left the team.

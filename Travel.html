San Fran

San Francisco is a beautiful city with skyscappers around every corner and a new unexpected encounter with quite alternative characters on every street.

LA

LA is a beautiful city with a very relaxed atmosphere. Everyone seems happy and outgoing.

Santa Barbara

Santa Barbara is an amazing town with a Surfer atmosphere. The beach is beautiful and warm and there are seaside stalls that sell homemade crafts.

Vegas

Vegas is an amazing city with an amazing nightlife. The party never stops. It is extremely warm there during the summer. It reaches up to 45 degrees.

Future
